Instruction for the investment game

In this game, you will be paired with another participant like you. One of you will be selected at random to be Participant A; the other will be Participant B. You will learn whether you are Participant A or B prior to making any decision.

To start, both Participant A and Participant B receive {{ Constants.endowment }} tokens; Participant A can send some or all of his {{ Constants.endowment }} tokens to Participant B. Before B receives this amount, it will be multiplied by {{ Constants.multiplier }}. Once B receives the tripled amount he can decide to send some or all of his tokens back to A.

Each token in this game worths 20 points in this experiment.
