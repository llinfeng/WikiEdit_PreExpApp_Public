Setting

You are packing your backpack and have 12 items to choose from. Each item has a certain weight and value to you. You want to maximize the value of the items in your backpack. However, your backpack can only carry a limited weight of items (the capacity).

Your Task

You will play {{num_rounds}} rounds with different items and backpacks. In each round, the weight in pounds (lb) and value in points of each item will be presented to you in a table.

You can put as many items in your backpack as you like as long as the total weight does not exceed the backpack's capacity. Once you have made your choice, click the button "Fix your selection and finish round".
